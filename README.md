# InTooDeep (English)

> *"You're a dwarf who's dug too deep and awakened an ancient evil."*

**InTooDeep** is a 2D puzzle platformer with a unique weight mechanic, created by **Krasnal Shortworks**. You play as a dwarf whose greed led him to dig too deep, awakening an ancient evil. Your goal is to escape to the surface with as much gold as possible.

The game features a distinctive **weight system** ‚Äì every item collected affects the character's physics, making movement and jumping more difficult. You must constantly balance between greed and agility.

## Key Features

* **Dynamic Weight Mechanic:** Your inventory and collected gold directly impact movement physics. The more you carry, the harder it is to escape!
* **Pixel Art:** Hand-crafted, dungeon-themed visuals.
* **Exploration & Combat:** Overcome obstacles, fight enemies, and avoid traps on your way to the surface.
* **High Score:** A scoring system based on collected loot ‚Äì high risk, high reward.
